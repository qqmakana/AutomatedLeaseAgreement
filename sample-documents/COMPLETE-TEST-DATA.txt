STEP-BY-STEP TESTING INSTRUCTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Upload Documents
-------------------------
1. Open http://localhost:3000
2. Copy Section 1 → Save as "landlord-cipc.txt" → Upload as "Landlord CIPC Document"
3. Copy Section 2 → Save as "tenant-cipc.txt" → Upload as "Tenant CIPC Document"
4. Copy Section 3 → Save as "surety-id.txt" → Upload as "Surety ID Document"

STEP 2: Paste FICA Text (Alternative)
--------------------------------------
1. Copy Section 4 (entire FICA document)
2. Scroll to "Or Paste FICA Document Text" section
3. Paste the text
4. Click "Extract Data from Pasted Text"

STEP 3: Fill in Premises Information
------------------------------------
1. Fill in all fields in "Premises Information" section using Section 5 data

STEP 4: Fill in Lease Terms
----------------------------
1. Fill in lease duration, dates using Section 5 data

STEP 5: Fill in Financial Details
-----------------------------------
1. Fill in Year 1 financial details
2. Year 2 and Year 3 will auto-calculate
3. Fill in deposit and lease fee

STEP 6: Generate Lease
-----------------------
1. Click "Preview" to see the lease
2. Click "Export Lease" to download as text
3. Click "Export PDF" to download as PDF
